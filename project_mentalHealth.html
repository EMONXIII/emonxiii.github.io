Description

Dumpling is a mental health application that offers a helping hand by combining PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS, Y-BOCS assessments to track your mood, a supportive mental health chatbot for 24/7 guidance, and a Mindful Journal - all in one place to help you manage symptoms of depression, anxiety, stress, and OCD.

  • Mental Health Assessment Feature: Users can take standard questionnaires for determining Depression, Anxiety, Stress, and OCD levels. And also visualizes the users' past scores using line or bar charts.
  • AI Chatbot: An integrated chatbot constantly provides support regarding the mental health issues. It also guides the user to the possible depression /anxiety /stress /OCD level detection section of the app if needed.
  • Mindful Journal: Dumpling's Mindful Journal component transcends the traditional function of a diary.

Testing Outcome: The beta testing phase for Dumpling has been a success, with no serious usability issues and users responding positively to the app's design, functionality, and overall user experience. Some users recommended minor improvements, such as Psychiatric help, more mental health specific chatbot.

Dumpling's mental health app provides users with complete tools for tracking their mood, receiving help, and practicing mindfulness by incorporating professionally proven evaluations, a supportive mental health chatbot, and a Mindful Journal. This comprehensive method enables people to effectively manage the symptoms of depression, anxiety, stress, and OCD. Dumpling demonstrates technology's ability to improve mental health care and general quality of life.
